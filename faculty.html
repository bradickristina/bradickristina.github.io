Faculty of Economics, University of Belgrade

Faculty of Economics and Business at the University of Belgrade is an educational and scientific institution heading to its 80 th anniversary. It was established as the Graduate School for Economy and Trade as far back as 1937 as the first higher educational centre in the field of Economics in the former Kingdom of Yugoslavia .
Today, the Faculty of Economics and Business is the biggest and the most prominent scientific and educational institution in the country in the field of economic sciences. The Faculty employs about 130 teachers and associates, of whom 53 are full tenure professors. Every year there are about 20 graduates and about 20 visiting scholars engaged in teaching. Up to now, around 46000 students have graduated from the Faculty; about 3000 have continued their studies successfully advancing to the MSc degree and about 760 acquired the PhD degree.

    The curriculum of regular studies at the Faculty comprises nine courses:
  • Economic Analysis and Policy
  • Finance, Banking and Insurance
  • Marketing
  • International Economics and Foreign Trade
  • Management
  • Accounting, Auditing and Financial management
  • Statistics, Informatics and Quantitative Finance
  • Trade Management and Marketing
  • Tourism and Hotel Management

It also offers Master, Doctoral and Specialist study programs.
